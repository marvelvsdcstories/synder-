About SKILLATRON


Our goal is to help students identify and develop some of the most important skills required to earmark their position in the field of Computer Science.The first edition of our event in the previous Consortium/Concoct was a huge success with a footfall of over 120 students.This Year,moving on from GUI applications,We plan to further enrich the participant's experience with a whole new user interface(UI) and a specially developed online platform.


Our event/contest will test, improve and reward the most important skills in students, required to become a professional in the stream of Computer Science(in specific) and the Industry(in general).It will emphasize on testing and building the skill set required to get into the corporate world, which we hope will inculcate a spirit of active learning and generate a pulse-cycle of competitiveness,effort and achievement in the participants.


What's our speciality?


DIVERSITY: We see that every participant is made to dive into both teamwork and sole-effort to make him/her recognize their respective importance.He/She will also learn to adapt to new team members,previously little known.This is important as in the practical world, co-workers would be from diverse cultures and geographical locations and adjusting with them and helping each other coexist to create a better end-product is important.


TIME MANAGEMENT: The participant will be posed with situations in which critical thinking is required to be implemented to solve problems/answer questions.This will greatly help the mind to start thinking in new ways,leaving behind the preset pattern that our regular mind follows towards solving a problem. He/She will also have to manage the given time well and also make quick decisions so that he will always stay in a position to win.
